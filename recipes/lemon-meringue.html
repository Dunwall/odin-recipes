Lemon Meringue Pie

Description

Lemon meringue pie is a tangy and sweet dessert with a crisp crust, smooth lemon filling, and fluffy meringue topping.

Ingredients

  • Pie Crust
  • Eggs
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees Celsius
  2. Prepare the pie crust and bake it
  3. Cook lemon filling with sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and butter
  4. Whisk eggs and add them to the lemon mixture
  5. Pour the filling into the pie crust
  6. Top with meringue and bake for 15 minutes
