Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a delicious and classic treat, with a perfect balance of chewy and crispy textures!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cream together butter and sugar
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract
  3. Mix in flour
  4. Fold in chocolate chips
  5. Scoop dough onto baking sheet
  6. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 10-12 minutes
